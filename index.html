FEATURES

BAREBONES houses a blend of functionality with its Feed, Search, Upload, Notes, and Profile tabs, offering a seamless user experience. Each workout post comes with a customizable template, allowing users to copy, paste, sort, and save notes straight from posts.

All-In-One Fitness Social Media App

Our platform amalgamates the world of fitness with the interactivity of social media, creating an all-encompassing space for fitness enthusiasts and certified trainers alike. Connect, share, and grow together in your fitness journey with BAREBONES.

Customize Your Workouts

Our platform allows users to tailor their own workout routines, offering the freedom and flexibility to build a fitness plan that aligns with individual goals. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned athlete, BAREBONES turns your workout aspirations into achievable actions.

Creator-Centric Subscription Service

BAREBONES takes a creator-focused approach, offering a subscription service that benefits both fitness enthusiasts and professional trainers. By subscribing, users gain access to exclusive content, unique workout plans, and expert advice from certified trainers.

Our Story

BAREBONES was born out of a passion for fitness and a vision to connect individuals on their health journeys. We saw a gap in the fitness market for an interactive platform that combines the power of social media with the practicality of a fitness app. Our aim was to build a community that promotes shared learning, growth, and a love for fitness.
