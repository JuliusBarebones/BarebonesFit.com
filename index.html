Fitness Without the Distractions

FEATURES

Barebones houses a blend of functionality and seamless user experience. Allowing users to share their workouts, connect with others, and build their own fitness community. Content is easily customizable and allows users to categorize and save workouts directly to their phone and save for later.

All-In-One Fitness Social Media App

Barebones incorporates the world of fitness with the interactivity of social media, creating an all-encompassing space for fitness enthusiasts and certified trainers alike. Connect, share, and grow together in your fitness journey with BAREBONES.

Customize Your Workouts

Barebones allows users to tailor their own workout routines, offering the freedom and flexibility to build a fitness plan that aligns with individual goals. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Barebones turns turns your workout aspirations into achievable actions.

Creator-Centric Subscription Service

Barebones takes a creator-focused approach, offering a subscription service that benefits both fitness enthusiasts and professional trainers. By subscribing, users gain access to exclusive content, unique workout plans, and expert advice from certified trainers.

COMMUNITY

Barebones allows individuals to charge for premium content. Subscribers will have exclusive access to workouts, meal plans, Q/A, & more. Apply now and create your own community!

How it works

  • Create a BAREBONES account
  • Apply to become a BAREBONES athlete
  • Submit your credentials
  • Get verified

The details

  • Once you are verified, you will receive a designated icon to showcase your community status.
  • You can build out a subscribable interface to engage with your followers.
  • Subscription fees are entirely up to the discretion of content creator.

OUR STORY

Barebones was born out of a passion for fitness and a vision to connect individuals on their health journeys. We saw a gap in the fitness market for an interactive platform that combines the power of social media with the practicality of a fitness app. Our aim was to build a community that promotes shared learning, growth, and a love for fitness.
